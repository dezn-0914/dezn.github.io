"Frieren: Beyond Journey's End" is a fantasy manga about an immortal elf mage who journeys to understand humanity after defeating the Demon King. The story is praised for its unique premise, beautiful art, and thought-provoking themes about life and death. The author, Yamada Aoi, is known for their previous work "The Ancient Magus' Bride" and is skilled at creating emotionally resonant stories with captivating visuals.




  
"Kaoru Hana wa Rin to Saku" is a heartwarming manga about a young woman finding healing and love through flower arranging. The story is praised for its beautiful art and exploration of themes like self-discovery and the power of nature. Author Natsuki Takaya, known for "Fruits Basket," creates relatable characters and heartwarming stories.




  
"How Do We Relationship?" is a heartwarming manga about two college girls who start a relationship of convenience that blossoms into something real. The story is praised for its sweet romance and relatable characters. The author, Tamifull, is known for their charming art style and ability to create heartwarming stories about everyday life.
